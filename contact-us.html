Frequently Asked Questions

What is AI?
AI (Artificial Intelligence) is the simulation of human intelligence in machines. It allows them to learn, solve problems, and make decisions. AI is widely used in healthcare, finance, and entertainment.
How can I listen to your podcasts?
You can listen on Spotify, Apple Podcasts, Google Podcasts, or our website. Some episodes are also available in video format on YouTube.
Are your podcasts free to listen to?
Yes, all podcasts are free. Some platforms may require an account for extra features like ad-free listening. We also offer a premium plan with early access to new episodes.
Can I download episodes to listen offline?
Yes, you can download episodes on Spotify and Apple Podcasts for offline listening. Keep in mind that downloads may expire based on platform policies.
How often do you release new episodes?
We release new episodes weekly, covering AI, technology, and innovation. Special episodes and mini-series are announced on our website and social media.
